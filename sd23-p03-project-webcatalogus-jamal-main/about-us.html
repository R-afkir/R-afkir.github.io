About us

founder Krishna Jamal

Hi, my name is Krishna Jamal and I am the proud founder of Project Webshop. Project Webshop is an online platform where students can buy and sell new and gently used school supplies at affordable prices. As a college student myself, I understand the struggles of trying to make ends meet while also being a diligent student. That is why I created Project Webshop to help my fellow students save money on necessary school items. Our platform is user-friendly and convenient, making it easy for students to find exactly what they need. I am excited to introduce Project Webshop to my peers and I hope it will make a positive impact on the student community. Thank you for your support!


co-founder: Redouan Afkir

My name is Redouan Afkir and I am a student at a local university. I am excited to introduce to you all our latest project, a webshop that I am proud to say I am a co-founder of. Our webshop offers a wide range of products, from clothing and accessories to home decor and tech gadgets. We have put a lot of thought and effort into curating a diverse and high-quality selection of items for our customers. I believe that our webshop will provide a convenient and enjoyable shopping experience for all who visit. I am looking forward to sharing more about our project with you all and hope that you will consider checking out our webshop for yourself. Thank you for your support!
